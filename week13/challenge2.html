Infinite Space, Right?

In Earths Orbit there are approximately 44,336 tracked satellites. These range from small bits of debris to the International Space Station. This large number of satellites create an ever growing problem: How are we suppose to keep these objects from colliding? Our purpose is to create awareness of this problem and create a fun an interesting way to track the satellites that crowd our planet.
